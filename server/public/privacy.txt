Privacy Policy

RestCue (the "App") respects your privacy. This simple privacy notice describes what information the App may collect (if any), how it is used, and the controls you have.

1. Information collected
- Authentication: When you sign in with Google or create an account, authentication is managed by Firebase Authentication. We do not collect or store your password; Firebase manages credentials.
- Optional profile info: your display name, email address, and profile photo provided by your identity provider.

2. How we use data
- To identify you and associate your saved check-ins and preferences with your account.
- To provide and improve the App's features and to restore your data across devices.

3. Third-party services
- Authentication is handled by Google/Firebase. Please review their privacy policies for details on how they process your data.

4. Data retention
- Account profile data is retained as long as your account exists. Local demo accounts are stored in your browser's localStorage and can be removed by clearing site data.

5. Contact
- For questions about privacy, contact the project owner.
